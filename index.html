Scholarly & Creative Work

Projects

Noctoflora: Last Light 2025

3D Sidescrolling Game — Master's Thesis

A thesis project developed in Unity 6 exploring what happens when humanity tries to perfect nature instead of living within it. Served as sole designer, developer, and 3D modeler, integrating game mechanics with critical media scholarship on digital preservation and authorship.

Mapping Seminal Works in the Digital Humanities 2026

Network Analysis & Visualization

A Gephi-based computational network analysis of 23 major texts in the Digital Humanities canon. Using modularity measurement, centrality measures, and clustering algorithms, the project identifies and visualizes three areas of overlap: shared scholarly themes, author networks, and disciplinary intersections.

Procedural Obsolescence In Progress

Doctoral Dissertation

Examines how digital licensing frameworks allow intellectual property holders to maintain perpetual control over cultural artifacts while simultaneously disclaiming responsibility for their long-term preservation. Drawing on case studies in video game history, the dissertation interrogates the legal, cultural, and archival tensions inherent in digital ownership.
